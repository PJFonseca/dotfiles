Turn Back Date
A specific deadline, occurring before the course start date, by which a customer must take action on a reserved seat. By this date, the customer must either assign a student to the seat, lock the seat for their exclusive use, or release (turn back) the seat without incurring any charges. If the customer does not act by the turn back date, the seat is automatically considered confirmed and will be invoiced, regardless of whether a student has been assigned. After the turn back date, the seat cannot be returned, and the Academy will not attempt to reassign it or offer it to waitlisted students. This deadline ensures operational certainty while allowing customers a degree of flexibility in managing their seat reservations up until that point.
