Glossary

A
Area of knowledge
Specific body of knowledge that identifies the course category and supports the identification.
Last Updated: 2025-03-27
Attendance
A measure of the number of students who attend a course iteration either in physical or virtual form. Students who did not complete the course or needed to withdraw once starting the course are considered to have attended a course iteration but have not graduated.
Last Updated: 2025-03-28
B
Blended learning
Multiple delivery types are used within the bounds of achieving the same course objectives, most commonly using self-paced online as a precursor to live online, Mobile Training Team (MTT), or face-to-face.
Last Updated: 2025-03-29
C
Ceased Training
Students are enrolled in an iteration and wish/need to cease attending the course iteration once it is started for any number of reasons: (Medical / Compassionate / Aptitude / Attitude).
Last Updated: 2025-03-30
Certification
Attendance at an Academy course may entitle students to a certificate of attendance, a certificate of completion, or a certificate of qualification as detailed in the individual course descriptions in the catalogue.
Last Updated: 2025-03-31
Course
Planned, sequenced, and structured learning activities based on objectives, which stem from E&IT requirements, for a clearly identified audience.
Last Updated: 2025-04-01
Course Code
Unique course identifier.
Last Updated: 2025-04-02
Course Control Document
(CCD)
A series of three course governing documents (Course Control Document (CCD) I, II, and III) that provide details of a course uploaded into the ETOC that meet a NATO E&IT requirement.
Last Updated: 2025-04-04
Course Graduate
See Graduate.
Last Updated: 2025-04-05
Course Iteration
See Iteration.
Last Updated: 2025-04-07
Course completion
Means that a student has completed an iteration of a course to the required standard and the provider of the course has recorded the outcome.
Last Updated: 2025-04-03
Customer
A NATO nation or group of nations (to include NATO Partner Nations when approved by the NAC) or a NATO entity who is the budget holder and has the authority to obligate and expend funds.
Last Updated: 2025-04-08
Customer Hosted Training Event
An instructor travels to the customer’s site to deliver a complete iteration of a course.
Last Updated: 2025-04-06
D
Delivery Method
Delivery method refers to the way academic content is conveyed from instructors to students in a course.
Last Updated: 2025-04-09
Department Head
(DH)
An officially recognized volunteer institution appointed by HQ SACT who is responsible to maintain a Discipline E&T programme.
Last Updated: 2025-04-10
Discipline
A NATO approved body of knowledge and skills, which supports an existing or evolving capability.
Last Updated: 2025-04-11
E
Education and Individual Training Solution
See Course.
Last Updated: 2025-04-12
Education and Training Activity
See NATO Education and Training Activity.
Last Updated: 2025-04-13
Essential Qualification
An E&IT qualification that is required of an Allied member prior to filling a NATO post.
Last Updated: 2025-04-14
Evaluation
The process of making judgments. A structured process of examining activities, capabilities, and/or performances against defined standards or criteria.
Last Updated: 2025-04-15
F
Face-to-Face
Students and instructor are physically present in the same NCI Academy training facilities, at the same time (synchronously).
Last Updated: 2025-04-16
G
Graduate
A student who has met all the requirements of the course.
Last Updated: 2025-04-17
H
Hybrid learning
The instructor is physically present in one of the NCI Academy training facilities, together with a number of students.
Last Updated: 2025-04-18
I
Iteration
The term given to the repetition of planned, sequenced, and structured learning activities based on objectives.
Last Updated: 2025-04-19
L
Level of Proficiency
A scale that differentiates the level of task, skill, and knowledge required for the job and will affect the type and amount of instruction required.
Last Updated: 2025-04-20
Live Online
Students and instructor are in different geographical locations and connect to the same digital training environment at the same time (synchronously).
Last Updated: 2025-04-21
M
Mobile Training Team
(MTT)
An instructor travels to the customer’s site to deliver a complete iteration of a course.
Last Updated: 2025-04-22
N
NATO Approved Course
DCOS MDFD certifies that a course open to NATO meets NATO-Specific E&IT Requirements and the ETF providing the solution is accredited.
Last Updated: 2025-04-24
NATO Education and Training (E&T) Activity
The delivery and conduct of specific individual and collective solutions to satisfy NATO-specific E&T requirements.
Last Updated: 2025-04-23
NATO Provided Course
A course that is programmed and delivered to meet the specific needs of NATO, categorized as compliant, and certified.
Last Updated: 2025-04-25
P
Prerequisites
All students are expected to meet the prerequisites for the courses attended as specified in the catalogue, including security clearance and language proficiency.
Last Updated: 2025-04-26
Q
Qualification
A formal result of a judgment and validation process where an authorized institution determines that individual learning output complies with defined standards.
Last Updated: 2025-04-27
R
Request ID
Unique identifier of the request made by the student.
Last Updated: 2025-04-28
S
Seat Status
Different status options for students' seats in the Training Management System.
Last Updated: 2025-04-29
Self-paced online
Learning activity that is not led by an instructor where students access online learning materials at a time of their own choosing.
Last Updated: 2025-04-30
Student
A named person who is enrolled to undertake training on an NCI Academy course.
Last Updated: 2025-05-01
Students trained
Number of students trained, which can include multiple student course records.
Last Updated: 2025-05-02
T
Training Coordinator
(TC)
The Academy focal point for all discussions regarding the training and has the appropriate authority to manage courses, iterations, and seats.
Last Updated: 2025-05-03
Training Management System
(TMS)
An online system available from any internet-capable computer or mobile device for managing training requirements.
Last Updated: 2025-05-04
Training Provider
The training provider refers to the Organization or Company delivering a course.
Last Updated: 2025-05-05
Trial
The activity of delivering a test version of a course and evaluating/vetting the content, activities, and assessment prior to the course ‘going live’.
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
W
Withdrawal
A withdrawal happens when a student had been enrolled in an iteration and needs to forfeit taking part before it has started.
Last Updated: 2025-05-07
